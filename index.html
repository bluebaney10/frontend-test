HOW DO I TRACK MY ORDER?

Once your order has shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this number on our website or the carrier's site to track your package.

CAN I MODIFY OR CANCEL MY ORDER?

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 1 hour of placement. After that, the order enters processing and changes may not be possible. Please contact customer service immediately.

WHAT PAYMENT METHODS DO YOU ACCEPT?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers. All transactions are securely processed.

HOW LONG DOES SHIPPING TAKE?

Standard shipping takes 5–7 business days. Express shipping is available at checkout and typically arrives within 2–3 business days.

DO YOU SHIP INTERNATIONALLY?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping times vary by destination, typically 7–14 business days. Duties and taxes may apply.

IS FREE SHIPPING AVAILABLE?

Free standard shipping is available on all orders over $150. This applies to domestic orders only. International orders have separate shipping rates.

WHAT IS YOUR RETURN POLICY?

Items can be returned within 14 days of receipt, provided they are unworn, unwashed, and in original condition with tags attached.

CAN I EXCHANGE FOR A DIFFERENT SIZE?

Yes, we offer free size exchanges on all full-price items. Please contact our customer service team to arrange an exchange.

ARE SALE ITEMS REFUNDABLE?

Sale items are final sale and cannot be returned or exchanged unless they arrive damaged or defective.

HOW DO I FIND MY SIZE?

Please refer to our size guide available on each product page. Measurements are provided in both centimeters and inches to help you find the perfect fit.

WHAT MATERIALS ARE USED?

Material composition is listed on each product page. We primarily use premium cotton, silk, and sustainable blends. Care instructions are included with every garment.

DO YOUR ITEMS RUN TRUE TO SIZE?

Most of our items run true to size. However, some styles may have a relaxed or slim fit. Check the product description for specific fit notes and recommendations.
